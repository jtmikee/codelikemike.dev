About Me

Hey! I'm Michael Campbell and I'm a Front End Web Developer based in Toronto. My passion for tech and creativity stem from my past where I created and customized spaces on the internet for myself as a teenager, refining skills or learning new technologies when I felt I had reached plateaus in the challenges and skill level required to build what sparked the excitment we all chase within our creativity.

With a background in sales and visual merchandising I strive to combine my creativity with my ability to provide services that exceed the standard and create an enjoyable experience for everyone involved. I'm dedicated to creating functional, creative, responsive and accessible applications and taking on challenges to always improve and refine my abilities to create exciting user interactions.
